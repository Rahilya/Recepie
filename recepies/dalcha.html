Dalcha Recepie

Dalcha is a Hyderabadi dal based dish that is not only delicious but is equally healthy. It has 3 major variants that are dal, mutton and a vegetarian one. This recipe is for a vegetarian Dalcha made using bottle gourd and Chana dal.

Ingredient

  • 1 cup channa daal
  • 2 tomato
  • 2 green chillies
  • 1/4 cup drump stick
  • 2 handful corinder leaves
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 table spoon red chilli powder
  • 2 table spoon mustard oil
  • 1/2 bottle guard
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind
